MUSION

Musion is an idea I developed in CI-102-3: Computing and Informatics Design. Musion connects people with similar music tastes.

Musion is an idea I ideatied and further developed with a team in CI-102-3: Computing and Informatics Design. The goal of Musion is to connect college students or young adults with others in their local area with similar music tastes, as well as provide local concerts in their preferred genre.

RENT A STUDIO

Rent-a-Studio is an idea my team created in EAM-212: Arts Management. The company provides space / materials to various artists.

Rent-a-Studio is a company a team and I brainstormed in EAM-212, a strategic arts management class. The company would provide accessible and afforadable studio space and materials to various artists, such as musicians, dancers, analog artists and digital artists.
